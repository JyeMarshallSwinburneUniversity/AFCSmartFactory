Australian Clothing
Smart Factory

The Australian Fashion Council, Epson Australia and the NSW goverment have commissioned
a feasibility study for a clothing smart factory.

Cycle 1

The first cycle is about understanding the market, the maturity needed and the business needs for a smart factory. A series of focus groups and follow up semi-structured interviews

Cycle 2

The second cycle Conceptualise a smart factory production plant including advanced simulation.
